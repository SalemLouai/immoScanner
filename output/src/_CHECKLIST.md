## Notes

- Items 58–64 (tests) are intentionally unticked. Per the pipeline contract, tests are the Tester agent's responsibility. All tested classes have no `NotImplementedException` and are fully functional.
- Item 37 (Polly): resilience is registered on HttpClients via `Microsoft.Extensions.Http.Resilience` (the .NET 9 recommended approach, superseding the legacy `Polly.Extensions.Http`). The DVF HttpClient gets retry + circuit-breaker pipelines through the standard handler pipeline.
- The solution builds with **0 warnings, 0 errors** under `TreatWarningsAsErrors=true`.
